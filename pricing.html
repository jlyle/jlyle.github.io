FAQ

Questions worth asking before you buy.

Why is your pricing higher than [cheap online resume mill]?

Because I'm not running a content farm. Every resume I write involves a real intake call, real research into the target roles, and 8–14 hours of actual writing. If price is the deciding factor, I'm probably not the right fit — and that's fine.

Do you write resumes outside of IT?

No. I'm an IT lifer — Navy, Red Hat, 25 years on the inside. I'd be a worse fit than a generalist for, say, a marketing director. Stay in your lane is good advice for me too.

What's the money-back guarantee, exactly?

If after the intake call and first draft you don't think we're a fit, email me within 14 days of the draft delivery. Full refund — no questionnaires, no exit interview. I'd rather lose the sale than have a client who isn't getting value.

Do you offer veteran or service-member discounts?

Yes — 15% off any package for all veterans and service members across every branch: Army, Navy, Marine Corps, Air Force, Space Force, Coast Guard, and their reserve and National Guard components. Active-duty, separated, and retired — all qualify. Just check the box on the order form; I'll verify after.

What if I just want a quick review?

Skip the packages — book a 30-minute resume review for $99. I'll record it and email you a transcript with everything I'd change. No obligation to do more.
